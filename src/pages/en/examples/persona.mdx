# The personas of this site

Understanding who the site is aimed at is essential to guide the tone, content and structure. Here are two personas representative of my target audience.

---

## üë© Claire - CIO in a large company

**Role:** Chief Information Officer  
**Organization:** Large company or group  
**Goals:**

- Successfully carry out complex digital projects
- Guarantee the quality and sustainability of solutions
- Rely on competent and reliable service providers

**Obstacles:**

- Lack of time to frame projects in detail
- Difficulty finding trustworthy technical interlocutors
- Desire to avoid generalists or "too artisanal" freelancers

**What she's looking for:**

- An experienced and autonomous technical expert
- A rigorous and professional approach
- Concrete references and measurable results
- Clear speech, without unnecessary jargon

**What can convince her:**

- Presentation of real cases, missions and methodology
- Serious, structured tone, impact-oriented
- Availability and responsiveness

---

## üë® Marc - HR Manager in an SME or a local authority

**Role:** Human Resources Manager  
**Organization:** SME, public or territorial organization  
**Goals:**

- Train his teams in digital
- Accompany technical transitions
- Work with accessible and pedagogical partners

**Obstacles:**

- Distrust of overly theoretical training
- Fear of choosing the wrong service provider
- High mental load

**What he's looking for:**

- A human, clear, and attentive interlocutor
- Concrete training, adapted to his teams
- A relationship of trust, easy to establish

**What can convince him:**

- Client testimonials
- Clear presentation of training offers
- An easy and pressure-free first contact

---

These profiles guide my content, tone and design choices on this site.
